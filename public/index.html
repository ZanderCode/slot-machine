Frogger Slots

Pull the lever, watch 'em spin, and hope that you get a clear path of lillies across! This slot demonstrates the modularity and customizability of the slots. It also demonstrates my understanding computer science concepts like recursion, since a path must be found. You can add any game logic onto any slot. When a slot finishes, grab the resulting matrix of symbols, reward the player based on what they got, change state, or even move a silly little frog across the screen!
